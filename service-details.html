Abstract Watercolor Flow

3 Hours Studio A Beginner Friendly

About this Experience

Dive into the fluid world of watercolors. This workshop is designed for those who want to let go of perfection and embrace the beauty of mistakes. You will learn wet-on-wet techniques, color blending, and how to create ethereal landscapes from your imagination.

All materials are provided, including premium cotton paper, professional-grade pigments, and high-quality brushes. Complimentary herbal tea and artisan pastries are served during the break.

What You'll Learn

  • Color Theory & Mixing
  • Wet-on-wet Application
  • Creating Texture with Salt
  • Composition Basics
Instructor: Sarah Jenkins

Professional Watercolorist with 10+ years of exhibition experience across Europe.
